About me

I’m Théophile Sauffisseau, a French engineering student focused on AI for healthcare — EEG analysis, computer vision, and biomedical imaging. I’m currently in a dual-degree between Télécom SudParis and the Institut Polytechnique de Paris.

I like building end-to-end projects: from data collection & cleaning, to modeling (mostly PyTorch), evaluation, and simple UIs to make results tangible. I won a first-prize student challenge with an Alzheimer’s detection from EEG project and completed an MRI segmentation internship. On this site I share a few selected works.

If you’re curious, start with my projects, browse the posters, or grab my resume. I’m looking for a 5–6-month R&D internship from April 2026 (med-tech/health preferred, open to high-impact domains).
